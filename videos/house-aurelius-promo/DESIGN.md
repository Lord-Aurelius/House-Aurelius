# House Aurelius Promo Design

## Source
Captured from https://house-aurelius.vercel.app on 2026-04-30.

## Style Prompt
Light, polished business infrastructure for African operators: soft lavender canvas, deep royal purple panels, warm gold highlights, rounded product cards, and a calm sun-mark motif. The video should feel secure, connected, and premium without becoming abstract or overly cinematic.

## Colors
- Canvas lavender: `#F6F2FF`
- Soft panel lavender: `#F0E8FF`
- Primary purple: `#2A1144`
- Secondary purple: `#3A1060`
- Product purple: `#6F24A7`
- Aurelius gold: `#F3CC6B`
- Warm cream: `#FFF9EA`
- White: `#FFFFFF`
- Muted body: `#5D4A73`

## Typography
- Primary: `Segoe UI`
- Weights: 300, 400, 600, 700, 800, 900
- Use heavy display weight for the brand name and gold-highlighted product words.

## Visual Elements
- Rotating sun mark from `capture/assets/svgs/brand-sun-icon.svg`
- Hero image of African professionals working with technology.
- Platform image cards for HAES, HAPOS, HARE, Church-lib, and Hifathi.
- Rounded panels and compact pill CTAs inspired by the website.

## Motion
- Smooth slide-down and fade-in inspired by the captured site.
- Slow sun rotation, subtle card drift, and Ken Burns motion on images.
- Scene transitions use soft focus crossfades and gold light sweeps.

## What Not To Do
- Do not use generic blue SaaS styling.
- Do not replace brand purple and gold with unrelated gradients.
- Do not make the promo feel like a landing page walkthrough.
- Do not obscure the real product categories or Africa-focused positioning.
