# Storyboard

## Beat 1: Brand Gateway, 0.0-5.2s
Hero photo fills the background with a deep purple overlay. The sun mark rotates slowly while the brand title rises into place. Gold underline and pill label establish the product as a unified business platform.

Assets: `photo-1531482615713-2afd69097998.jpg`, `svgs/brand-sun-icon.svg`
Transition: incoming scene 2 overlays with a soft blur/fade and gold sweep from right to left.

## Beat 2: Connected Verticals, 4.8-10.2s
Five platform cards slide into a clean grid. Each card shows the real captured preview image, product code, and industry. A central gold line implies one gateway connecting all cards.

Assets: HAES, HAPOS, HARE, Church-lib, Hifathi preview photos.
Transition: incoming scene 3 overlays with focus pull and scale.

## Beat 3: Purpose-Built Operations, 9.8-15.2s
Three metric panels appear: 5 platforms connected, 5 business verticals, aggregated metrics only. Supporting labels reinforce privacy-aware public dashboard data.

Assets: product preview photos as soft background tiles.
Transition: incoming final scene overlays with a calm crossfade.

## Beat 4: Secure Across Africa, 14.8-20.0s
Return to the hero visual with a refined product lockup. Final CTA and contact-style footer line appear over a premium purple panel. End with a gentle fade to purple.

Assets: hero photo, sun mark.
Transition: final fade only.
